For Sellers

Download Sameep

Download the Sameep application from the official app store for your device

Register as a Seller

Launch the Sameep app and sign up as a seller.

Create Seller Profile

After successful registration, log in to the Sameep app as a seller. Fill in your seller profile information, including your shop name, GST number, address, and other relevant details.

List Products

Select products from the existing database by browsing categories and subcategories, or alternatively, add new products manually. Provide necessary details for each product, such as title, description, images, pricing, and stock availability.

Order Processing and Payment

When you receive an order notification, review the details, including the customer's shipping address and the products ordered. Prepare the order by packaging the products securely for delivery. Wait for Sameep's designated delivery agent to pick up the order from your specified location. After successful delivery, the customer's payment will be processed by Sameep, and you will receive your payment for the order according to the agreed-upon payment terms.
